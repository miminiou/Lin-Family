Lin Family

About 有關這個網頁

Continuing the spirit of my parents, Chingfu and Hsiumei Lin, and their lifelong effort in family relationships, this website is meant for their family members to connect and support one another. They both lived a great lives that demonstrated how important family is and how family members should connect, encourage, support, and enjoy time with one another. Hopefully, this website may bring you greater understandings of your family linkage and bring you encouragement and joy concerning your heritage.

承繼先父母親， 林慶福先生和林張秀美女士，畢生對家族關係貢獻的精神，這個網頁的首要目的是要幫助家人彼此聯繫和互相鼓勵。我父母親燦爛的一生活出了家庭的重要性，和家人應該彼此聯繫，鼓勵，幫助和享受聯誼的榜樣。期望，這個網頁能幫助你更多認識你在這個家族裡的關係，並因這個家族而激勵你，帶給你喜樂。

- Pao-Hwa Lin 林寶華
